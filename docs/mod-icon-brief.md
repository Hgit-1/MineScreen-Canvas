# MineScreen mod icon brief

Use case: logo-brand

Asset type: Minecraft/NeoForge mod icon, square PNG

Primary request: an original compact emblem for MineScreen, combining a block-shaped world screen with a subtle connected-tile grid

Style/medium: crisp pixel-art-inspired game icon; flat, readable shapes; polished at 512×512 and still recognizable at 32×32

Composition/framing: one centered near-square screen block with a strong charcoal frame; the lit display is divided into a subtle 2×2 joined-panel grid; generous safe margin

Color palette: deep charcoal and near-black frame, electric cyan display, one warm high-contrast yellow accent matching the in-game control indicator

Constraints: no words or letters; no Minecraft grass block; no browser/vendor marks; no gradients that become muddy at small sizes; no mockup, background scene, watermark, bevel clutter, or photorealism

Intended output: `src/main/resources/minescreen.png`, 512×512 PNG. After generation, validate at 64×64 and 32×32, then add `logoFile="minescreen.png"` to `neoforge.mods.toml`.
